Paint Layout Tool

All paints have a layout that affects how they are displayed. The paint layout can stretch, rotate and otherwise change how the paint is drawn on-screen. This tool provides an easy way to manipulate the paint layout.

Some paints (such as flat colors) will not be visibly altered by their paint layout.

There are three different sub-tools for laying out paints:

Linear Paint Layout

  • Provides a simple line that defines the start and end points of a linear gradient.

Radial Paint Layout

  • A more complex control that lets you position the center, radii and focus of a radial paint.
  • Pressing <ctrl> and dragging will let you position the focus point even if it is hidden by the other drag handles.

Texture Paint Layout

  • Lets you position a texture fill by manipulating the bounding quad of a single texture unit.
